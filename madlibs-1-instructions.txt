Madlibs Kata: 1 Replaced Word 

1. obtain a sentence string (sentenceString)
2. split string at spaces into an array of words (sentence)
3. for testing, write sentence array to console log
4. obtain index of word in sentence array to replace - first is 0 (index)
5. obtain part of speech of word to replace (partOfSpeech)
6. set word at this index equal to underscore + partOfSpeech + underscore
7. join array of words into new sentence(newSentence)
8. display new sentence as madlib.

Madlibs Kata 2: Author and Player. 

9. display message: "Author, get player.  You leave now."
10. obtain a word that matches the part of speech required (replacement)
11. replace the element in sentence that had the part of speech with the replacement word
12. join sentence array of words into new sentence (newSentence)
13. display message: "Player, get author,  When both here, see Madlib."
14. display new sentence.
